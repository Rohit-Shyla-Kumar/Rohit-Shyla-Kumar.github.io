What I love doing

AI for General FPS

As a part of my final year project, I'm working on a bot that can play more than one particular game. Since I grew up mostly playing first person shooters, I decided that would be the genre my bot would play as well. As of today, the system plays DOOM (1998) better than the average human player.

Game Development

I've developed four games for courses and recreationally on everything from the Unity engine to C++ with the SDL library. 

The Pineal - Aid for Visually Impaired Bowlers

Computer vision and image processing application that assists blind bowlers via an Android application. Won the Technologies for the Elderly and Disabled Makeathon and the Best of the Best Awards for Best Innvoation of 2017 at the City University of Hong Kong. Currently funded by the Jockey Club Youth Empathy Project.

Computer Vision in Action

I interned at Horus Intellisys in Bangalore for 14 weeks where I worked on the team developing India's first Advanced Driver Assistance System. I was also the project leader for a proof of concept of a drive through application for McDonald's and worked on an application to make bus stops safer in India. 

AI in Music

I worked a 10 week internship at the Composers and Authors Society of Hong Kong on AI platforms for lyric generation and text classification.  

Web Development for Access

I've designed, developed and launched 3 websites for the School of Law of my University, including their career portal where firms can list openings, a site for a Law Review and a blog for distingueshed members of the law community of Hong Kong.

Public Speaking

Over the last decade I've devoted a large portion of my free time to literary activities such as debating, acting and Model UN. I've won dozens of such events all of world including the Harvard Model UN Conference in India, the 21st Student Leader Convention in Singapore and even the World Federation of the United Nations Association's Model UN in New York.

Data Science for India

Data Science for India is a program based at UC Berkeley seeking to further promote the interdisciplinary field of data science in India through outreach at secondary schools and universities. Since it's inception in 2017, I've been a curriculum developer and was the Regional Director for Bangalore during the 2017 Summer program. 

Social Entrepreneurship

To end female foeticide in India and the rest of the world, I'm working on a system that uses image processing and artificial intelligence to perform the sensitive healthcare procedures during which a child's gender may be discovered.
